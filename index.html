A House Full of Love

A wet nose nudge, a purring sound,
Happy moments, all around,
A playful chase, a gentle lick,
These furry friends, a loving trick,
Helped her forget college stress and strife.

Graduation came, a bittersweet day,
Leaving her pets, far away,
But the memories, they'll always stay,
Of dogs and cats, that light her way,
